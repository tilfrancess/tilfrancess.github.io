Dining Experience & Fun

Few little secrets to our "Miracle"
Restaurant & Bar pair.

  • 20th January, 2023
  • Team VillaFiori

The practice of eating in hotels is no longer reserved for tourists who are too sluggish to explore the city. Dining in a hotel has evolved into a "gourmet" experience, a luxury enjoyed by those who enjoy fine cuisine and beverages. Whether you're staying at our hotel for work or pleasure, you can treat yourself to delectable meals and drinks at the Miracle Bar and our outstanding Miracle Restaurant. Enjoy beverages and cigars in the exquisite bars as you entertain and network with business contacts or treat a loved one to a wonderful dining experience.

The Miracle restaurant is available for lunch from 12:00 to 16:00 and dinner from 19:00 to 23:00, with an a la carte menu that focuses entirely on regional delicacies and cuisine.

The Miracle restaurant offers a la carte menu items that are all based on regional dishes and cuisine and are lovingly and expertly prepared by our chefs. The restaurant is available for lunch from 12:00–16:00 and supper from 19:00–23:00. The Miracle Bar is open around-the-clock and is equipped to handle any order, from a straightforward espresso to a mixed cocktail.

Before you explore the lovely Montepulciano, our team will greet you with a delicious breakfast in a dining space that has been particularly built and offers a wide variety of products made in the local Italian region. Fresh, well curated, and lovingly prepared continental fare and drinks. Our multilingual staff will kindly greet you and provide you with any information you may need.

Peculiar Italian Cuisine

A dining experience at Miracle Restaurant provides a window into the culinary traditions of Italian cuisine. A La Carte menu options include daily homemade regional specialties including pizza and pasta, as well as desserts, fruit, and vegetables. Along with some of your old favorites, you're likely to discover a brand-new dish that you'll enjoy.

A magnificent cuisine that will educate you about the customs of our country and enchant you with its singular and ravenous flavors. Improvement of the ingredients, warmth, and soul for a kitchen and dining room staff with significant impact.

Miniature Taste of Tuscany

Each dish is a miniature taste of Tuscany, made even better by the wine pairing. The fact that they are only created with products from the property, together with meat, cured meats, and cheeses that come from nearby organic farms, ensures the seasonality and authenticity of the ingredients.

Our restaurant is ideal for birthday celebrations, wedding anniversaries, and romantic dinners. All vistas on the complex's terraces are open and well-intended, with some pointing to some Suites' private terraces and others to the complex's shared pool, gardens, and terraces. These enable you to enjoy the first spring sunshine and cool summer evenings while living comfortably in the countryside nearby and taking in the natural world's seasonal color changes. As soon as the weather permits, breakfast, lunch, and supper are served al fresco on the terrace while being soothed by the sounds of nature.

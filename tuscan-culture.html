Vineyard & Wine Tasting Course

Tuscan wine is making its way to someone's heart.

We will spend 4 hours exploring Montepulciano and its vineyards and olive trees within the famous landscape of the Tuscan hills. Our trip to Avignonesi will take us along a cypress-lined road that leads to Le Capezzine, the heart of the 200-hectare estate that includes vines in Montepulciano and Cortona.

We'll go through a beautiful vineyard, exploring organic vines, maturation cellars, and Avignonesi's famed Vinsantaia, as well as sampling local wines and products. We will learn about the winemaking process as well as the materials used to make the barrels.

We then return to our hotel to discuss and try 5 different Tuscan wines, including Rosso di Montalcino and two different types of Brunello Docg, a Super Tuscan and a lovely Rose.

The course will end with sample local products such as cheese, salumi, olive oil and balsamic vinegar from our kitchen.

Meet our chef's team for a 6 hours authentic culinary journey across gourmet Tuscany. Learn the seasonal flavors of Tuscan cuisine and taste the region's best wines. Discover how to make real handmade pasta, bruschetta, Tuscan roast pork with potatoes, and Tiramisu with simple and fresh ingredients from a local chef. Put on your chef hat and apron and get ready for a fantastic cooking experience.

Start by making your own Italian fresh pasta by hand with freshly laid eggs and learn to make a traditional meat sauce using ingredients. Learn how to make a traditional bruschetta using fresh bread, garden tomatoes, and organic virgin olive oil.

You will learn how to make classic Tuscan roast pork, and Tristan and Tuscan roast potatoes with fresh herbs. Once they are ready move on to learn the secrets of cream dessert, the Tiramisu using all the provided ingredients and finding out which tools are needed.

After you've finished cooking, take a break and relax in our garden with a carefully chosen bottle of wine from the region to complement the flavors of the meal you've prepared.

Bike Tour

Private Guided
Bike Tours

Discover the Wonders of Montepulciano with us!

The Montepulciano neighborhood is great for those who enjoy cycling throughout the city. Embark on your journey through Tuscany's picturesque hills and olive gloves, vineyards.

The tour's highlights include Montefollonico, Pienza, and Montepulciano. Our professional tour guide will teach you about each location's impressive history, architecture, and culture. The guided bike tour includes classic Italian bicycles, helmets, water bottles, and Tuscan delicacies.

LIVE MUSIC Every evening, our young musicians will play a high-end nylon string classical guitar under the stars in our garden. Our guests will experience a journey through Italian music in a variety of styles and periods. The ambiance will be intimate since it is one of the charactericts that makes the classical guitar unique. This is the ideal moment for couples to celebrate a special and romantic occasion under the stars with unique musical tones. Free of charge to our guests.

OPEN AIR CINEMA Classic movies in the tranquility of Tuscan hills. Every week, from 7 p.m. to 10 p.m., the Villa Fiori team organizes Italian-themed movie evenings in our vineyard outdoor theater. Settle down on chairs under the stars to watch an old-time classic Italian film surrounded by the most peaceful and luxury ambiance, with the tranquility of the countryside in the background and the wavy shapes of the wooded hills in the distance. With fully free entrance, you can take a seat and enjoy the movies that are presented on each occasion. The open-air cinema screenings begin as the sun sets behind the hills, giving way to darkness and moonlight. The most iconic films in Italian cinema history will be shown one after another, lighting the summer evenings with the beauty of black and white. As you settle in to watch your movie while admiring the starry sky, enjoy the delicious popcorn and, upon request, the elegant wine service. You can book private chefs which is ideal for a romantic anniversary date night or honeymoon! Don't worry if it rains; we'll have umbrellas available.
